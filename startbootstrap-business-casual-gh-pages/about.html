Dream It - Paint It - Wear It About Scribble Customs

Back in 2016 while working at Foot Locker during high school, I realized how integral shoes were in a person's style. From the runners looking for nothing but functionality, to the hypebeasts who care about nothing but the exclusivity, everyone needs a good pair of shoes they can lace up or look at daily.

I guarantee that you will be able to enjoy your customs everyday. The time and care placed into each individual shoe satisfies the quality requirement from avid runners while invoking the same sense of exclusivity that hypebeast are always in search of.
