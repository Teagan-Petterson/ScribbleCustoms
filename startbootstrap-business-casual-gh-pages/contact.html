Dream It - Paint It - Wear It The Process

Bring your imagination to life with a pair of your own custom shoes. Contact me to design yours today!

Whether you consider yourself creative, or not, I will take the time to get to know you and what you are interested in so that I can design the best shoes for you. I will then purchase the base shoe, paints, and any other materials required for the customs, annd start the painting immediately. In order to guarantee the highest quality, the process can take up to 6 weeks, but has taken as little as 5 days.

I guarantee that you will be filled with a child-like giddiness from the moment you unbox your first pair, to the moment that you retire them, just to start the cycle again with your next pair.
